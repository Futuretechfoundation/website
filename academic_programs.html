Programs and Courses

Future of Humans Track

This track focuses on advancing human capabilities through biotechnology, 3D bioprinting, nanorobotics, and more.

  • Biotechnology: Covers genetic engineering, synthetic biology, and bioinformatics.
  • Regenerative Biotherapeutics: Develops therapies for tissue regeneration using stem cells.
  • 3D Bioprinting: Creates complex biological structures, advancing personalized medicine.
  • Prosthetics: Focuses on artificial limbs with enhanced integration.
  • Nanorobotics: Studies nanoscale robots for medical applications.
  • Surgical Robotics: Uses AI-driven robotics to enhance precision in surgeries.

BCI and Humanoids Track

Explores the interface between human cognition and AI, with a focus on digital consciousness and humanoid robotics.

  • Brain-Computer Interface (BCI): Enables communication from brain signals to external devices.
  • Telepathy-Based Communication: Uses BCI to facilitate mind-to-mind communication.
  • Neural Networks & Brain Simulation: Develops AI models to replicate human thought.
  • Mind Uploading: Investigates transferring consciousness into digital or robotic forms.
  • Humanoids Development: Focuses on AI-driven robots that mimic human movement and cognition.

Quantum and Space Tech Track

Combines quantum computing, non-human vehicle technology, and interdimensional travel.

  • Quantum Computing: Uses quantum mechanics for advanced computation.
  • Quantum Materials Engineering: Develops new materials at the atomic level.
  • Brain-Computer Interface Integration: Merges human thoughts with quantum computing.
  • Mind-Controlled Vehicles: Enables vehicles to respond to human thoughts.
  • Interdimensional Travel: Investigates high-speed travel across dimensions.
