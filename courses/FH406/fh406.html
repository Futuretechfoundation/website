Welcome to Cryonics in the Modern World

Spring 2025

This comprehensive 8-week course explores cutting-edge cryonics principles, applications, and future frontiers. Through interactive modules, virtual labs, and expert guidance, you'll gain hands-on experience with the latest cryopreservation techniques, legal and ethical considerations, biological challenges of revival, and much more.

Introduction to Cryonics

Welcome to the Cryonics Course! This course will explore cryopreservation techniques, legal and ethical considerations, biological challenges of revival, and much more. Complete the quizzes at the end of each section to earn your certificate!

Key Areas Include:

  • Advances in vitrification and cryoprotectants
  • Neuropreservation versus whole-body preservation
  • Legal and ethical considerations, including the definition of death and consent
  • Biological challenges of tissue repair and neuron viability
  • Future technologies like nanomedicine and AI
  • Social and philosophical implications, including identity and resource allocation
  • Organizations like Alcor and Cryonics Institute, and the cost of preservation

Course Modules:

Introduction

Cryonics is the science of preserving individuals at extremely low temperatures after death, with the hope of future revival.

Quiz: Introduction to Cryonics fundamentals

Reversible Cryopreservation

Learn about technologies that allow freezing and thawing of complex organs, enabling organ transplantation and whole-body revival.

Quiz: Understanding reversible cryopreservation techniques

Advancements in Cryoprotectants

Explore non-toxic cryoprotectant solutions that prevent freezing damage, ensuring better preservation of cells and tissues.

Quiz: Cryoprotectant development and applications

Cryonics and Aging Research

Discover how cryonics integrates with anti-aging and regenerative medicine to extend lifespans.

Quiz: Integration of cryonics with aging research

Global Collaboration and Standards

Learn about efforts to establish international cryonics standards for procedures and safety.

Quiz: International standards in cryonics

Space Cryonics

Learn about using cryonics for long-term space travel and its role in interstellar exploration and planetary preservation.

Quiz: Applications of cryonics in space exploration
