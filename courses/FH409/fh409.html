Welcome to Next-Gen Space Technologies
Spring 2025

This comprehensive 8-week course explores cutting-edge space technology principles, applications, and future frontiers. Through interactive modules, virtual labs, and expert guidance, you'll gain hands-on experience with the latest innovations shaping humanity's future in space.

The course covers the Artemis program's lunar missions, Mars exploration efforts, advanced spacecraft propulsion systems, satellite technologies, space habitats, life support systems, and emerging concepts like space manufacturing and artificial gravity.

By the end of this course, you'll understand the technical challenges and opportunities in modern space exploration, and be prepared to contribute to this rapidly evolving field.
