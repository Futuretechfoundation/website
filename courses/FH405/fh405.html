Welcome to Advanced Surgical Robots

Spring 2025

This comprehensive 8-week course explores cutting-edge surgical robotics principles, applications, and future frontiers. Through interactive modules, virtual labs, and expert guidance, you'll gain hands-on experience with the latest surgical robotic technologies and understand their impact on modern medicine. From the da Vinci system to emerging autonomous platforms, this course covers the full spectrum of surgical robotics innovation.
