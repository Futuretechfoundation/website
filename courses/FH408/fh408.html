Welcome to Building the Future of Space Habitats

Spring 2025

This comprehensive 8-week course explores cutting-edge space habitat design principles, technologies, and future possibilities. Through interactive modules, virtual labs, and expert guidance, you'll gain hands-on experience with the latest innovations in space construction, life support systems, and sustainable off-world living environments.

You'll learn to address the unique challenges of designing habitable structures for low Earth orbit, lunar surfaces, Mars, and beyond. By the end of this course, you'll have developed practical skills in space habitat conceptualization, planning, and problem-solving that prepare you for careers in the expanding space industry.
