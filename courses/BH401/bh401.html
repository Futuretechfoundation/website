Welcome to Brain Computer Interfaces for the Future
Spring 2025
This comprehensive 8-week course explores cutting-edge brain-computer interface (BCI) technologies and applications. Through interactive modules, virtual labs, and expert guidance, you'll gain hands-on experience with the latest advancements in neurotechnology, from non-invasive EEG systems to revolutionary invasive interfaces developed by companies like Neuralink and Synchron.
Discover how BCIs are transforming medicine, communication, and human enhancement. Learn about the technical foundations, current applications, and future possibilities of direct brain-computer communication. This course provides both theoretical knowledge and practical insights into one of the most exciting frontiers in neuroscience and engineering.
