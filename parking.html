Parking and Transportation

Parking Options

Our campus provides multiple parking options to accommodate all visitors, staff, and students.

  • General Parking: Available to all visitors. Located near the main entrance. Fee: $5 per hour.
  • Staff Parking: Reserved for staff with a valid permit. Located in the north and south lots.
  • Student Parking: Available to students with a permit. Located near the residence halls and library.
  • Accessible Parking: Available near all major entrances for visitors with a valid permit.

Transportation Options

There are various transportation options to make it easier for you to get to and around campus.

  • Campus Shuttle: Free for students and staff. Runs every 15 minutes between major campus locations.
  • Bike Rentals: Available at bike stations located across campus. Rentals start at $3 per hour.
  • Public Transit: Conveniently located bus stops near the main entrance and residential areas.
  • Ride Sharing: Designated pick-up and drop-off zones available for ride-sharing services.
