Our Mission

We are dedicated to pioneering surgical robotics through open research, collaboration, and ethical innovation. Our focus includes haptic feedback systems, neural control, force sensing, image-guided navigation, and miniaturized in vivo surgical platforms.

Research Projects

  • Force Feedback System: Real-time haptic feedback for improved surgeon awareness.
  • Macro-Micro Coordination: Harmonizing large-scale robotic arms with micro-surgical tools.
  • Image-Guided Navigation: Integrating AI-assisted visualization for high-precision targeting.
