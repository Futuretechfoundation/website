FutureTech Foundation Joins OpenBCI Community

Date: December 4, 2024

We are proud to announce that FutureTech Foundation has officially joined the OpenBCI community!

This marks a significant step forward in our commitment to advancing neurotechnology and fostering open collaboration within the field. By aligning with OpenBCI, we aim to contribute to groundbreaking innovations that empower researchers, developers, and enthusiasts around the world.

The OpenBCI community is renowned for its dedication to exploring the frontiers of brain-computer interface (BCI) technology and making it accessible for a wide range of applications. As part of this vibrant ecosystem, FutureTech Foundation looks forward to sharing our expertise, learning from peers, and co-creating solutions that shape the future of human-computer interaction.

Stay tuned for updates as we embark on this exciting journey!

Future Tech Foundation Joins the e-NABLE Hub

Exciting news from the e-NABLE Hub! The Future Tech Foundation has officially joined the community and received their first badge, marking a significant step toward becoming a certified e-NABLE Community Chapter member.

This badge represents their commitment to the mission of empowering individuals and communities by creating innovative, 3D-printed prosthetic devices for those in need.

Learn more about Future Tech Foundation's achievement and the e-NABLE Hub.

FutureTech Foundation Achieves 501(c)(3) Status

Date: August 24, 2024

FutureTech Foundation has officially received 501(c)(3) status, enhancing our ability to partner with other nonprofits and accept tax-deductible donations. This is a significant milestone that will help expand our community-driven projects and volunteer initiatives.

Regenerative Medicine Minnesota (RMM) 2025 Grant Application

Date: October 20, 2024

FutureTech Foundation has submitted an application under the RMM Discovery category. Our focus is on scalable iPSC production for neurodegenerative and musculoskeletal disease therapies, showcasing our commitment to advancing regenerative medicine.
