Current Focus: C. difficile Project

We are engineering DNA nanorobots to identify and neutralize Clostridioides difficile infections.

  • Targeting C. difficile toxins with stimuli-responsive nanorobots
  • Delivering antimicrobial peptides and enzyme inhibitors
  • Fluorescent signal output to confirm delivery

Scaffold used: M13mp18 DNA for stable and programmable nanostructures.
