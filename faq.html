Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of Futuretech Foundation?

Futuretech Foundation is a community-based organization focused on volunteering, technological education, and future technology mentorship. We aim to inspire innovation through technology in diverse communities.

What types of courses do you offer?

We offer courses in various future-focused fields such as VR and AR, quantum programming, 3D bioprinting, nanorobotics, humanoid robotics, regenerative biotherapeutics, and more.

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

We welcome volunteers in various roles, including teaching, mentorship, and community outreach. For those interested, please contact our volunteer coordinator for more information.

Is Futuretech Foundation a registered nonprofit organization?

Yes, Futuretech Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and is enrolled in E-Verify.

Are Futuretech Foundation and Futuretech Academy the same entity?

No, Futuretech Foundation is a nonprofit organization, while Futuretech Academy operates as a for-profit entity. Both work collaboratively to provide educational and technological resources to our community.

How can students access Futuretech courses?

Our courses are available online, with login management handled by web3 wallets. The courses cover topics in VR/AR, quantum computing, biotechnology, and more, accessible through our web3-compatible platform.
