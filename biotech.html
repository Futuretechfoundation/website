This comprehensive course explores cutting-edge biotechnology principles, applications, and future frontiers. Through interactive modules, virtual labs, and expert guidance, you'll gain hands-on experience with the latest biotechnology tools and techniques.

From genomics and precision genetic engineering to synthetic biology and regulatory considerations, this course covers the full spectrum of modern biotechnology applications.

Week 2: Genomics and Precision Genetic Engineering

Learning Objectives

  • Explain advanced genomic technologies and their applications
  • Understand CRISPR-Cas9 and other gene editing systems
  • Analyze implications of genomic medicine and personalized healthcare

Key Activities

  • Virtual CRISPR laboratory simulation
  • Genomic data analysis workshop
  • Ethics case study: gene editing applications

Recommended Preparation

Review basic principles of DNA structure, replication, and gene expression. Familiarize yourself with the general concepts of PCR and DNA sequencing technologies.

Week 3: Molecular Biology and Advanced Biochemistry

Learning Objectives

  • Master advanced molecular biology techniques
  • Understand protein engineering and structural biology
  • Apply biochemical principles to biotechnology applications

Key Activities

  • Virtual molecular biology laboratory practicum
  • Protein structure prediction exercise
  • Design challenge: Engineering an enzyme for specific applications

Virtual Lab Access

Access to the virtual molecular biology lab will be available starting Monday of Week 3. Make sure your BioSimulations Pro account is set up before lab sessions.
