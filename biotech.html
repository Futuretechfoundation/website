Course Syllabus

Welcome to FH402: Biotech: Next-Level Applications

This comprehensive 8-week course explores cutting-edge biotechnology principles, applications, and future frontiers. Through interactive modules, virtual labs, and expert guidance, you'll gain hands-on experience with the latest biotechnology tools and techniques.

From genomics and precision genetic engineering to synthetic biology and regulatory considerations, this course covers the full spectrum of modern biotechnology applications.

Course Highlights:

  • Virtual laboratory simulations for hands-on experience
  • AI-powered tutoring and personalized feedback
  • Industry expert sessions and real-world applications
  • Capstone project tackling current biotechnology challenges

Week 2: Genomics and Precision Genetic Engineering

Learning Objectives:

  • Explain advanced genomic technologies and their applications
  • Understand CRISPR-Cas9 and other gene editing systems
  • Analyze implications of genomic medicine and personalized healthcare

Key Activities:

  • Virtual CRISPR laboratory simulation
  • Genomic data analysis workshop
  • Ethics case study: gene editing applications

Week 3: Molecular Biology and Advanced Biochemistry

Learning Objectives:

  • Master advanced molecular biology techniques
  • Understand protein engineering and structural biology
  • Apply biochemical principles to biotechnology applications

Key Activities:

  • Virtual molecular biology laboratory practicum
  • Protein structure prediction exercise
  • Design challenge: Engineering an enzyme for specific applications

Week 4: Medical Biotechnology and Therapeutic Applications

Learning Objectives:

  • Evaluate current and emerging biopharmaceuticals
  • Understand cell and gene therapy approaches
  • Analyze the drug development pipeline for biologics

Key Activities:

  • Biotherapeutic development simulation
  • Virtual cell culture and bioprocessing laboratory
  • Regulatory strategy workshop for a novel therapeutic

Week 5: Synthetic Biology and Bioengineering

Learning Objectives:

  • Design biological systems with engineered functionalities
  • Apply principles of synthetic biology to solve real-world problems
  • Understand emerging tools in biomaterials and bioengineering

Key Activities:

  • Design challenge: Creating a synthetic biological system
  • Metabolic pathway engineering simulation
  • Bioprinting virtual laboratory

Week 6: Emerging and Interdisciplinary Areas

Learning Objectives:

  • Identify convergent technologies reshaping biotechnology
  • Understand the integration of AI and data science with biotechnology
  • Explore nanobiotechnology and biomolecular engineering

Key Activities:

  • Machine learning workshop for biological data analysis
  • Nanobiosensor design challenge
  • Multi-omics data integration exercise

Week 7: Biotechnology Policy, Regulation, and Ethics

Learning Objectives:

  • Navigate the regulatory landscape for biotechnology products
  • Analyze ethical frameworks for evaluating emerging biotechnologies
  • Understand intellectual property considerations in biotechnology

Key Activities:

  • Regulatory strategy development exercise
  • Mock ethics review board
  • Intellectual property workshop

Week 8: Final Project & Future Frontiers

Learning Objectives:

  • Synthesize knowledge across multiple areas of biotechnology
  • Develop a biotechnology solution to a real-world challenge
  • Evaluate emerging trends and future directions in biotechnology

Key Activities:

  • Capstone project finalization and presentation
  • Peer review session
  • Virtual biotechnology innovation showcase

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Apply advanced biotechnology concepts to solve real-world problems
  • Design and analyze genetic engineering strategies
  • Evaluate ethical, social, and regulatory implications
  • Integrate multi-omics data using computational approaches
  • Develop strategies for translating research into applications
  • Navigate the biotechnology commercialization landscape
