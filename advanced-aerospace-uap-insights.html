Lesson 1: Metamaterial Design for UAPs

Metamaterials allow groundbreaking manipulation of electromagnetic properties, such as:

  • Permeability Control: Precise manipulation of magnetic permeability for specific frequencies.
  • Permittivity Adjustment: Engineering dielectric substrates to enhance electric field responses.
  • Magnetoelectric Coupling: Interaction between magnetic and electric fields for added control.
  • Field Distribution Asymmetries: Creating localized spacetime distortions with tailored gradients.

Fabrication techniques include:

  1. Photolithography: Patterning substrates for nanoscale structures.
  2. Self-Assembly: Leveraging molecular organization for uniform designs.
  3. 3D Printing: Additive manufacturing for intricate geometries.
  4. MEMS Actuators: Real-time tuning of resonator gaps for dynamic responses.
